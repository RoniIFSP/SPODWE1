Resoluções dos Exercícios de Flexbox e Grid CSS

Lista de seletores corretos para os desafios do Flexbox Froggy e do Grid Garden.

Flexbox Froggy - Resoluções

  1. Exercício 1: justify-content: flex-end;
  2. Exercício 2: justify-content: center;
  3. Exercício 3: justify-content: space-around;
  4. Exercício 4: justify-content: space-between;
  5. Exercício 5: align-items: flex-end;
  6. Exercício 6: align-items: center;
  7. Exercício 7: justify-content: center; align-items: center;
  8. Exercício 8: flex-direction: row-reverse;
  9. Exercício 9: flex-direction: column;
  10. Exercício 10: flex-direction: row-reverse; justify-content: flex-end;
  11. Exercício 11: flex-direction: column; justify-content: flex-end;
  12. Exercício 12: align-items: center; flex-direction: row-reverse;
  13. Exercício 13: justify-content: space-between; align-items: flex-end; flex-direction: column;

Grid Garden - Resoluções

  1. Exercício 1: grid-column-start: 3;
  2. Exercício 2: grid-column-start: 5;
  3. Exercício 3: grid-column-end: span 2;
  4. Exercício 4: grid-column-end: -2;
  5. Exercício 5: grid-column: 4 / span 2;
  6. Exercício 6: grid-row-start: 3;
  7. Exercício 7: grid-row-start: 2;
  8. Exercício 8: grid-row-end: -2;
  9. Exercício 9: grid-row-end: span 3;
  10. Exercício 10: grid-row: 2 / span 3;
  11. Exercício 11: grid-column: 2 / span 3; grid-row: 2 / span 2;
  12. Exercício 12: grid-template-columns: 50% 50%;
  13. Exercício 13: grid-template-rows: 25% 50% 25%;
